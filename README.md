## 🔥 Key Capabilities

### 🔍 Competitive Dissector
Simulates a scan of Google Top 10 results to identify content gaps, competitor weaknesses, and user intent patterns. It looks for "expert truths" that competitors are ignoring.

### 🏛️ GEO-Optimized Architecture
Every article follows a deep outline structure that prioritizes:
- **GEO Summary**: Instant, bolded answers for generative search models.
- **Information Gain**: Each section is designed to add unique value or data not found in existing results.
- **LSI & Semantic Injection**: Advanced keyword mapping for broad topical authority.

### 🛠️ Editor Micro-Surgery
A built-in QA node reviews every section for quality, data points, and "AI fluff." If a section fails, the AI performs targeted "micro-surgery" to fix only the problematic parts.

### 📈 Final Article Auditing
Automated scoring against three critical standards:
1. **On-Page SEO**: Title, H1, internal linking, and flow.
2. **EEAT**: Citations, expert tone, and unique perspective.
3. **GEO Performance**: Synthesis and information gain metrics.
